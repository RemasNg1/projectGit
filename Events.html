Preparing Future Medical Professionals
The Medical City at Qassim University welcomed students from the College of Applied Medical Sciences to provide practical and scientific support before their internship in the Laboratory and Blood Bank departments. This initiative aims to bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and real-world application, ensuring that students are well-prepared for their upcoming internships. During this program, students engaged in hands-on training sessions, gaining valuable insights into laboratory procedures and blood bank operations. The Medical City emphasizes the importance of practical experience in the healthcare field, equipping students with the necessary skills to excel in their future careers. This collaboration not only enhances their understanding of medical practices.
Nursing Month: Empowering Healthcare
Qassim University Medical City hosted an exhibition showcasing the vital role of nursing in healthcare. This event highlighted the significant impact that nursing has both locally and globally, emphasizing the essential contributions of nurses to patient care and health outcomes. The exhibition featured presentations from esteemed professionals, including the Medical Director and the Academic Affairs Manager, who discussed the evolving landscape of nursing and its critical importance in the healthcare system. Attendees included staff, students, and visitors who engaged in insightful discussions about the future of nursing and its challenges. Through this event, the Medical City aimed to raise awareness about the indispensable role of nurses in promoting health, advocating for patients, and shaping health policies. It served as a platform for networking and collaboration among healthcare professionals, fostering a deeper understanding of nursing's influence on healthcare delivery.
Blood Donation Campaign for Saudi National Day
The Medical City at Qassim University organized a blood donation campaign in celebration of Saudi National Day, held at the main campus. This initiative aimed to promote community health and encourage civic responsibility among students and staff. The campaign attracted a large number of participants, reflecting the community's commitment to supporting healthcare efforts. Medical professionals provided guidance and support throughout the event, ensuring that the donation process was safe and efficient. In addition to fostering a spirit of national pride, the campaign raised awareness about the importance of blood donation and its impact on saving lives. This event not only contributed to the local blood supply but also strengthened the bonds within the university community as they came together for a noble cause.
World Hand Hygiene Day Awareness Event
The Medical City at Qassim University, led by the Infection Control Department and nursing staff, organized an event for World Hand Hygiene Day to raise awareness about the importance of hand hygiene in preventing hospital-acquired infections. This event emphasized the critical role that proper handwashing plays in safeguarding patient health and reducing the spread of infections. During the event, healthcare professionals conducted interactive workshops and demonstrations, showcasing effective handwashing techniques and the use of hand sanitizers. Attendees were educated on the significance of maintaining hand hygiene in various healthcare settings and the impact it has on overall patient safety. By promoting a culture of hand hygiene, the Medical City seeks to enhance the quality of care provided to patients and to foster a safer healthcare environment for everyone involved.
World Physical Therapy Day Awareness Event
Qassim University Medical City held an event to raise awareness about knee osteoarthritis, led by the Physical Therapy and Rehabilitation Department. This initiative aimed to educate the community about the causes, symptoms, and treatment options for knee osteoarthritis, a condition that affects many individuals and can significantly impact their quality of life. During the event, healthcare professionals provided informative talks and demonstrations on exercises and rehabilitation techniques that can help manage the condition. Attendees were given the opportunity to participate in interactive sessions, where they learned about the importance of maintaining joint health and preventing further deterioration.
Medication Safety Awareness Series
The Pharmaceutical Services Department at Qassim University Medical City continues to hold a series of lectures on medication safety and the role of healthcare practitioners in the optimal use of medication. These lectures aim to enhance the understanding of safe medication practices among healthcare professionals and the community. Throughout the series, experts in pharmacology and clinical practice discuss various topics, including the importance of proper medication management, recognizing potential drug interactions, and ensuring adherence to prescribed treatments. Participants engage in interactive discussions, case studies, and Q&A sessions, fostering a collaborative learning environment. The lectures also emphasize the critical role healthcare practitioners play in educating patients about their medications, addressing concerns, and promoting informed decision-making. By providing this knowledge, the Pharmaceutical Services Department aims to reduce medication errors and improve overall patient safety. These ongoing educational efforts reflect the commitment of Qassim University Medical City to promote health literacy and ensure that both practitioners and patients are well-informed about the medications they use.
